Offline Mode

Download this site folder to your phone or computer, then open offline.html to access every generator and calculator in one place. Use the tabs below to switch tools without needing internet access.

How to save for offline use (underway/deployed): download the full folder, keep all files together, and open offline.html from your Files app or file manager. This works with no internet once the files are stored locally.
