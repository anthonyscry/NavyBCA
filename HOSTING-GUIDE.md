## üìà SEO Strategy (Get Found on Google)

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console (FREE)
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add your website
3. Verify ownership (add a meta tag or upload a file)
4. Submit your sitemap
5. Request indexing of your pages

### Step 2: Create a Sitemap
Create a file called `sitemap.xml`:

```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://yourdomain.com/</loc>
    <lastmod>2025-01-03</lastmod>
    <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
    <priority>1.0</priority>
  </url>
</urlset>
```

## üí∞ Monetization Options

### Option 1: Google AdSense (Easiest)
**Expected Revenue: $1-5 per 1,000 visitors**

**Requirements:**
- Original content
- Privacy policy page
- At least 20-30 pieces of content (recommended)
- Site must be 1-3 months old (sometimes)

**How to Apply:**
1. Go to [Google AdSense](https://adsense.google.com)
2. Sign up with your Google account
3. Add your website
4. Place the AdSense code in your HTML (I've added placeholders)
5. Wait for approval (1-14 days)

**Ad Placements in the Calculator:**
- Top banner (728x90)
- Between results (300x250)
- Bottom of page (728x90)
- Sidebar (if you add one) (300x600)

### Option 2: Ezoic (Better Rates)
**Expected Revenue: $5-15 per 1,000 visitors**

**Requirements:**
- 10,000+ monthly pageviews
- Original content

**Benefits:**
- AI-optimized ad placements
- Higher revenue than AdSense
- Free CDN and hosting

### Option 3: Mediavine (Best Rates)
**Expected Revenue: $15-30 per 1,000 visitors**

**Requirements:**
- 50,000+ monthly sessions
- Original, quality content
- Good standing with Google

**Example:** "Need a proper measuring tape? We recommend [this one on Amazon]"

### Option 5: Sponsored Content
