About This Site

A Retired Sailor's Quest to Never Plan Another Burpee

About This Site

As a retired Information Systems Technician Chief (ITC), coming up with ideas for Command PT was always a struggle. I served as a CFL or ACFL at every command I was stationed at, and took the CFL course four times in my career.

Week after week, I'd find myself scrambling to plan fresh, varied PT sessions while juggling my IT duties. It got old fast.

So I built this site—the BCA Calculator, PT Generator, and Command PT Watchbill Generator—to make life easier for Sailors still serving. These are the tools I wish I had when I was a CFL.

What's Here

BCA Calculator

  • Uses current Guide-4 (December 2025) standards
  • Two-step process: Waist-to-Height Ratio, then body fat percentage if needed
  • Shows clear pass/fail status
  • Uses official table lookups for exact body fat percentages
  • Saves your inputs for tracking progress
  • Mobile-friendly

PT Generator

  • Creates varied, NOFFS-compliant 60-minute PT sessions in seconds
  • Adapts to available equipment and location
  • Includes standardized warm-ups, main workouts, and cool-downs
  • Supports strength, cardio, mixed, Fun Day (team sports), and Chill Day (yoga) options
  • Print-ready format with PPAQ questions included

Command PT Watchbill Generator

  • Creates monthly PT and FEP schedules with calendar view
  • Automatically rotates CFL/ACFL assignments across all sessions
  • Blocks federal holidays - no PT scheduled on holidays
  • Customize PT days (default M/W/F) and FEP days (default T/Th)
  • Set default PT time and add custom locations (Base Gym, Track, Pool, etc.)
  • Per-session customization: override time, location, or assigned CFL
  • Remembers your roster, locations, and schedule defaults on this device
  • Performance metrics report showing total sessions and breakdown by CFL/ACFL
  • Print-ready format with full calendar and metrics for Navy evaluations

What I'm Working On Next

  • Shareable watchbill links and CSV/PDF exports for easier command distribution
  • Saved PT Generator preferences (equipment, location, focus areas) between visits
  • Command-specific holiday imports to avoid scheduling conflicts
  • Quick templates for common PT/FEP schedules

Why It's Free

I spent plenty of time in the Navy. I know what it's like balancing your actual job with CFL duties. I'm not going to charge Sailors for tools that help them meet mandatory requirements.

This site is completely free—no subscriptions, no logins, no paywalls. There's minimal advertising to cover hosting costs, but that's it.

Important: This is an unofficial planning tool. Official BCA measurements can only be conducted by certified CFLs/ACFLs. Always verify with your command's fitness program.
