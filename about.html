About This Site

Why I Built This

I built this site because planning Command PT week after week was a constant struggle. As a retired Sailor, I served as a CFL or ACFL at every command I was stationed at and took the CFL course four times in my career.

Week after week, I'd find myself scrambling to plan fresh, varied PT sessions while juggling my IT duties. It got old fast.

So I built this site for the Sailors still doing the job. I wanted something you could open, get an answer, and move on with your day — no spreadsheets, no guesswork, no extra drama.

What's Here

  • BCA Calculator: Fast pass/fail results using current Guide-4 standards, with saved inputs.
  • PT Generator: Ready-to-run 60-minute sessions with warm-up, workout, and cool-down.
  • Watchbill Generator: Auto-rotated CFL assignments, holiday blocking, and print-ready calendars.
  • 8-Week PRT Prep: A simple plan to build pushups, planks, and run endurance.
  • Offline Mode: Save the site and keep the key tools handy without an internet connection.
  • References: Quick access to the official PDFs you actually need.

What I'm Working On Next

  • Shareable watchbill links plus CSV/PDF exports
  • Saved PT Generator presets between visits
  • PRT score tracking and goal-setting dashboard

Why It's Free

I spent plenty of time in the Navy. I know what it's like balancing your actual job with CFL duties. I'm not going to charge Sailors for tools that help them meet mandatory requirements.

This site is completely free—no subscriptions, no logins, no paywalls. There's minimal advertising to cover hosting costs, but that's it.

Important: This is an unofficial planning tool. Official BCA measurements can only be conducted by certified CFLs/ACFLs. Always verify with your command's fitness program.
